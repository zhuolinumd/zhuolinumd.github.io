Currently I am a Scientist in Raytheon BBN Technologies. Before coming to BBN, I worked as a researcher in Huawei Technologies in Hong Kong from 2013~2014. From 2011~2012, I was an Assistant Research Scientist in the Computer Vision Lab at University of Maryland, College Park, where I was working with Prof. Larry S. Davis. My research interest includes computer vision and machine learning, specifically on sparse coding, dictionary learning, submodular optimization, supervised and unsupervised clustering, and transfer learning.
