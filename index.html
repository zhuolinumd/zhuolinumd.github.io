Zhuolin Jiang

I am a Principal Research Scientist at RTX Technology Research Center. Previously, I was a Senior Machine Learning Scientist at Emerald Innovations (2020~2024) and Scientist II at Raytheon BBN Technologies (2015~2020). I also worked as a Researcher at Huawei's Noah's Ark Lab (2013~2014) and as an Assistant Research Scientist in the Computer Vision Lab at the University of Maryland (2010~2012) with Prof. Larry S. Davis.

My research spans learning from vision, language, and RF sensing data, with recent work focusing on trustworthy large language models and agentic AI frameworks for multimodal understanding and reasoning.

Email zhuolinumd@gmail.com
